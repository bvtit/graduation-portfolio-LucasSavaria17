A Few More Things About Me...

If you're on this page that means you're curious to learn more about me, so I'll start by telling you a little more about my life. I have lived in Milford Massachusetts my entire life with my Mom, Dad, and my younger sister Abby. From a young age I always had a love for electronics and I would go out of my way to surround myself in them. Whether it was video games, or going inside of my Dad's old computers, they have always been an important hobbie of mine along with the list of my other hobbies on the Home page.

Through my three years spent in this shop it has given me many oppurtunities to learn more about the things that interest me the most, Computers. All the teaching I have recieved has allowed me to become well-educated in the field of IT; even to the point where I have built my own personal gaming computer. Shop has definitly been an important piece in my life that is helping me push forward towords my goal of working in Cyber Security when I graduate.

As for my aspiration for the future, my dream job would have to be working around the things I love the most, which is computers. Specifically Cyber Security is what I am interest in the most. I do believe that having some form of a College Education is very important to having a successful life and career, but my plans for out of High School is to jump right into the work force. I'm currently working towords my CCENT Certification and during my Senior year I plan to go for my CCNA. In order to get this far ahead of the game I plan on taking the Cisco III course over the summer in order to be able to complete Cisco IV during my final year of High School.
